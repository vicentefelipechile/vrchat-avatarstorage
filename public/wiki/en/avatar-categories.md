# How to Categorize Your Avatar

<span class="badge badge-blue">Guide</span> <span class="badge badge-yellow">Upload</span>

## Why does the category matter?

When you upload an avatar to VRCStorage, you are asked to assign it to a category. This helps other users find your content through filters and searches. An incorrect category doesn't ruin anything—the moderation team can correct it later—but a reasonable category helps your avatar be visible from the very beginning.

> [!NOTE]
> If you are unsure which category to use, **choose the closest one** and move forward. Do not block your upload because of this. Moderators regularly review content and reassign categories when necessary.

## Available Categories

| Category | Description | Typical Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Human** | Human-looking characters with natural or slightly stylized proportions. | Booth models (Selestia type), "Club-ready" avatars. |
| **Furry** | Anthropomorphic avatars. Includes styles from Toon to hyper-detailed with fur shading. | Foxes, wolves, bipedal dragons, felines. |
| **Anime** | Models that strictly use Cel-shading and aesthetics from Japanese series or visual novels. | VRoid styles, Japanese video game characters. |
| **Chibi** | "Short/Small" models. Not just exaggerated proportions, but designed to be short in-game. | Chibi dolls, nendoroids, extreme kawaii styles. |
| **Cartoon** | Non-human styles with flat or "Vibrant" shading. Simple shapes oriented toward expressiveness. | Fall Guys avatars, Telepurte, or "Rubber Hose" type mascots. |
| **Semi-Realistic** | Avatars with PBR (physically real) textures, detailed skin, pores, and clothing with complex materials. | MetaHuman models, 3D scans, Final Fantasy characters. |
| **Monster** | Non-human creatures focused on horror, the bizarre, or the imposing. They often use non-standard animations. | Avatars with other types of furry traits, Backrooms creatures, Elden Ring bosses. |
| **Fantasy** | Characters with mythical attributes that maintain a human or stylized aesthetic base. | Elves, Tieflings, Knights in full armor, Mages. |
| **Other** | Anything that does not fit into the previous categories. | Any type of avatar. |

## How to Decide When in Doubt

### Is it Furry or Human with accessories?
The key distinction is whether the character has **structural animal traits** in its base body:
* **Human**: Ears and tail as accessories (Kemonomimi) on a human body.
* **Furry**: Snout, fur covering the body, or animal paws designed into the mesh.

### Is it Anime or Human?
The difference lies in the artistic style and the shading (shader):
* **Anime**: Large 2D eyes, clean outlines, and use of *Toon Shaders*.
* **Human**: "Instagram model" proportions, skin textures with detail, and shading that seeks 3D depth.

### Is it Chibi or Cartoon?
* **Chibi**: Huge head, very small body, and Japanese "kawaii" aesthetic.
* **Cartoon**: Simplified shapes, flat colors, and Western cartoon aesthetic.

### Is it Monster or Fantasy?
* **Monster**: If the first impression is threatening, horrific, or bizarre (e.g., a mass of flesh with eyes).
* **Fantasy**: If it is a fantastic being with a heroic or elegant appearance (e.g., an elf or an aesthetic demon).

## Quick Decision Table

| If the avatar has... | Suggested Category |
| :--- | :--- |
| Snout, fur, and structural animal traits | **Furry** |
| Large eyes and Japanese drawing style (Cel-shading) | **Anime** |
| Large head, very small body, and "Bean" aesthetic | **Chibi** |
| Simplified shapes and cartoon aesthetic | **Cartoon** |
| Stylized human appearance (Booth/Gumroad style) | **Human** |
| Photorealistic detail or high-fidelity game textures | **Semi-Realistic** |
| Dark, deformed, or horror appearance | **Monster** |
| Classic fantasy being (elf, fairy, knight) | **Fantasy** |
| Is an inanimate object or an abstract concept | **Other** |

## What Happens if You Make a Mistake

Nothing serious. Categories are editable by the moderation team. If you upload an avatar with an incorrect category:
* The avatar remains visible and functional.
* A moderator can correct the category without you having to re-upload the model.
* You can correct it yourself from the edit page at any time.

> [!WARNING]
> The only thing that **does matter** is not using **Other** for everything. Reserve that category for cases where there is no other option. Using it as a wildcard makes it harder for others to find your content.
